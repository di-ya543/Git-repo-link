Features

What you can do

  • Instructors only: Monthly workshop count, upcoming workshops, profile stats, comments on coordinator profiles.
  • Open to all: Browse workshops, request or propose dates, search and filter by topic and region.
  • Coordinator actions: Accept/decline proposals and schedule an event in 2 taps on mobile.

How it works

Search > Select workshop > Propose dates or Accept > Confirm. The UI minimizes form fields on mobile and uses date-picker native controls for speed.

Instructors post details with required tags (topic, target audience, prerequisites). Posts are compressed on mobile with expand/collapse to keep listing lengths short.
